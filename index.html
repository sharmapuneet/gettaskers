Ratings & reviews

Review Tasker's portfolios, skills, badges on their profile, and see their transaction verified ratings, reviews & completion rating on tasks they’ve previously completed on Gettaskers. This empowers you to make sure you’re choosing the right person for your task.

Use Gettaskers to stay in contact from the moment your task is posted until it’s completed. Accept an offer and you can privately message the Tasker to discuss final details, and get your task completed.

Gettaskers Pay is the seamless and secure way to get your tasks completed. Once you accept an offer on a task, the agreed upon amount is held secure with Gettaskers Pay until the task is complete.
